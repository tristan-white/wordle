=====MENU=====
Choose a mode:

"interactive"
    Think of a 5 letter word and have the Wizard try to guess it while you give manual feedback. Enter "help feedback" to see syntax for feedback.

"auto"
    The Wizard tries to guess your word but feedback is given to the Wizard automatically.

"batch"
    Give the Wizard a list of words to guess. See statistics for the Wizard's performance.
